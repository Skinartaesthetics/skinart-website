Our Story

Skincare that's personal, not generic

Great skin does not happen by guessing.

At SkinArt Aesthetics, every treatment is built around your skin — not trends, not templates, and not a standard facial menu. Your skin has its own story, and it deserves a plan that actually makes sense.

Founded by Dina Telesh, a licensed esthetician with a medical background, international experience, and advanced certifications in Face Reality, Circadia, GlyMed+, and Zemits technologies, SkinArt was created for clients who want expert guidance, intentional treatments, and visible results without the pressure of unnecessary upsells.

Every visit starts with a detailed skin conversation and professional assessment. From there, we design a customized protocol using corrective facials, acne-focused treatments, professional peels, advanced modalities, and clinical skincare lines chosen specifically for your skin’s condition, goals, and tolerance.

No rushed appointments. No cookie-cutter facials. No guessing.

Just elevated, results-driven skincare with purpose — designed to help your skin become healthier, stronger, clearer, and beautifully refined over time.
